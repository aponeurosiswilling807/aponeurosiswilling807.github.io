## 🚀 Using the interface

The application dashboard shows your recent projects. To check a design, follow these steps:

1. Click the "New Review" button on the main screen.
2. Select the platform you want to target, such as iOS or visionOS.
3. Drag and drop your design files or screenshots into the window.
4. Click the "Analyze" button.

The app scans your files for common errors. It checks font sizes, button shapes, and color contrast. It also verifies your use of design tokens. The software produces a report with suggestions. You can export these reports as PDFs to share with your team.

## 🛠️ Features and utility

The software focuses on high-quality user experience.

* **Accessibility checks:** The tool identifies text that lacks contrast. It finds buttons that measure too small for touch.
* **Design token support:** The app maps your colors and spacing to current Apple standards.
* **Cross-platform guidance:** You receive tips for mobile and desktop layouts. It manages the specific nuances of visionOS and watchOS.
* **SwiftUI integration:** The plugin suggests code tweaks to match Apple design patterns.
* **Liquid Glass support:** The app provides feedback on how your elements handle transparency and blur effects.

## 💡 Troubleshooting common issues

If the application fails to open, restart your computer. If the analysis stalls, check your internet connection. Large image files may take longer to process. Ensure your files are in a standard format like PNG, JPG, or PDF. 

If the tool does not detect your design elements, try saving your design with fewer layers. Flattening your images often improves scan speed. If error codes appear, contact the support team through the GitHub issues page. Include the version number shown in the "About" menu.

## 🏗️ Managing design tokens

Design tokens act as the source of truth for your UI. This application imports JSON tokens to standardize your workflow. Use the "Settings" menu to link your token library. The app alerts you when your design deviates from these tokens. This keeps your interface consistent across all Apple devices. 

Follow these steps to update your tokens:

1. Open the "Settings" tab. 
2. Select "Token Management."
3. Click "Import Library."
4. Select your JSON file.

The application automatically validates these tokens against updated Apple documentation every time the software starts. This ensures you always build compliant interfaces.

## 🔍 Understanding report results

The analysis report uses a simple scoring system. Green marks indicate items that follow the guidelines. Yellow suggests improvements for better usability. Red marks areas that violate Apple standards. Expand each section to read the specific advice. The software provides direct links to the Apple documentation that supports each finding. This helps you understand the reasoning behind every suggested change. 

The software automatically saves your reports in the "Documents/Apple-HIG-Reports" folder. You can rename these files to match your project names. Use the search bar in the main app window to find past reviews by project name or date. This history tool helps you track your progress as you iterate on your UI designs.
